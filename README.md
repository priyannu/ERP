CampusERP – College ERP Management System

CampusERP is a fast, responsive, and full-stack college ERP system designed for students, faculty, hostel residents, and day scholars.
It centralizes all campus services—academics, hostel, attendance, library, assignments, fees, notices—into one smooth, lag-free web platform.

 Features
Student Portal
Personalized student dashboard
Attendance overview (daily/weekly/overall)
Semester results and grade history
Course registration with add/drop
Assignment upload & submission tracking
Fee details, dues, payment status
Notices, alerts & department announcements
Technical helpdesk for issue reporting
Hostel & Day Scholar Management
Hostel resident dashboard
Weekly mess food menu
Mess feedback, complaint logging
Outpass requests & approvals
Day-scholar transport pass info
Library System
Book search with filters
View issued books & due dates
Fine calculation
Online book reservation
 Dispensary / Medical
Health record overview
Medical visit history
Appointment booking
Emergency contacts
 Faculty Information
Department-wise faculty directory
Contact details & consultation hours
Subjects handled by faculty
 Notifications & Alerts
Real-time announcements
Academic updates
Emergency alerts
User-specific notifications
